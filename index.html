    Dragon Foils

    Leading manufacturer of cold and hot foil and a National High-tech Enterprise in China. It enjoys a high reputation in both domestic and overseas markets.

    • -Hot Stamping Foil Series

      Metallic & Holographic Hot Stamping Foil

      PP: Suitable for low surface tension, especially OPP laminated films.


      PD+: Great stamping coverage, ideal for rough paper, textured surfaces, and book bindings.


      BD: Overprintable hot foil. Good overprintability with strong ink adhesion.


      POP: Holographic hot foil, more than 40 holographic patterns, customized patterns and transparent-based holographic foil are available.

    • -Cold Foil Series

      Metallic & Holographic Flexo Cold Foil

      FXC: Top speed reaches 180m/min (Gallus ICS670), while typical speed ranges from 60-100 m/min on standard flexo printers with silver/gold foil.


      Metallic & Holographic Sheet-fed Offset Cold Foil

      OSC: Suitable for applications with conventional and UV glues.
      High speed stamping and excellent transferring quality, good for stamping with fine details, halftone and board area.
      Overprintable quality with high metallic gloss and good adhesion.
      Widely used in regular packages, tobacco packages, folding cartons, books, etc.

    • -Digital Enhancement Foil Series

      Metallic & Holographic Digital Enhancement Foil

      DT: Applicable for the digital printing machines equipped with varnish jetting and foiling part like SCODIX, with excellent adhesion.
      Good for both solid and fine details, stamping with sharp edge.
      Rich colors, high gloss with good smoothness property, strong 3D effect after stamping.
